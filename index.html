Welcome to DataPlot!

DataPlot is the prototype of a data visualization dashboard for display of aggregate end-user email view activity. DataPlot provides multiple settings for updating the dashboard visualizations. Timeframe data, population segment data, and device usage statistics are all displayed and can be selectively used to modify the displayed data.

  • Press the orange Trend Line button to toggle display of best-fit data trend line.
  • Choose the time-range of displayed data by pressing the desired blue button on the top right screen corner.
  • User breakdown by gender is displayed on the bottom left screen corner. Click on individual population segments in order to selectively display data from that population on the charts.
